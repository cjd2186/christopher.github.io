NYC Parking Ticket Data Querying

Completion: November 2020

This project leverages the Python Pandas library in order to ingest data from a csv file, allowing for many different statistics to be calculated regarding nyc parking ticket information.
Statistics calculated include:
* Amount of money owed given a plate number.
* The license plate that owes the most money.
* Year with most money owed in parking tickets
